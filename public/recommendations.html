Recommendations

Based on the current tourism asset distribution and gap analysis, here are some recommendations:

  • Promote underrepresented categories like eco-spots and accommodation.
  • Improve infrastructure in areas with fewer tourism assets.
  • Encourage community-based tourism projects.
  • Regularly update and verify tourism data for accuracy.
