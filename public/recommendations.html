Strategic Recommendations

Based on the current tourism asset distribution and gap analysis, here are some strategic recommendations to enhance tourism potential in the province.

Promote Underrepresented Categories

Focus marketing efforts and development incentives on asset types with low counts, such as eco-tourism spots and unique local accommodations, to diversify the region's offerings.

Enhance Infrastructure

Identify areas with a high density of attractions but poor supporting infrastructure. Prioritize road improvements, signage, and public utilities in these zones to improve visitor experience.

Encourage Community-Based Tourism

Develop and support community-led tourism projects. This empowers local populations, provides authentic experiences for tourists, and ensures that economic benefits are distributed more widely.

Maintain Data Integrity

Implement a quarterly review process to update and verify the tourism asset data. Accurate, up-to-date information is crucial for effective planning and for providing reliable information to visitors.
